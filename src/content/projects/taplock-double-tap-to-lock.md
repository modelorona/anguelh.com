This is an Android application that lets the user add a resizeable transparent widget anywhere on their homescreen. When they double tap it, their screen locks. It requires the accessibility permission in order to lock the screen, and collects no data nor does it have any metric or usage tracking libraries.
<br /><br />
I created this out of annoyance that the Google Pixel devices do NOT have this feature. My LG G3 from 2014 and Samsung S10E from 2019 both had it. Meanwhile Google ignores their users' requests and people have to resolve to using 3rd party applications.
